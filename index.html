Weather Station Monitor

Explore weather stations from around the world and view their historical data.

Features

  • Interactive Map: Click on station clusters to zoom in and explore individual stations
  • Station Details: Click any station marker to view detailed information and weather history
  • Historical Data: View temperature, wind, pressure, and precipitation data over time
  • Clustering: Stations are grouped by proximity for easy navigation

How to Use

  1. Zoom and pan the map to explore different regions
  2. Click on blue cluster circles to zoom into a region
  3. Click on individual station markers to view detailed weather data
  4. Scroll through the historical data table to see weather trends
